United Hospitality Management is a Privately Owned Hospitality Ownership and Management Company

Over 75 Years Experience

We are a family owned and operated company focused on investing, developing, and managing both full and select-service hotel properties. The principals of United Hospitality Management have over 75 years of combined experience in hotel and real estate investment, development, and management. Our partners currently own and operate a portfolio of hotel properties and are always seeking new investment and development opportunities.

Hands-On Hotel Management

We pride ourselves in achieving both exceptional guest satisfaction and employee relations. United Hospitality Management has been successful in creating a culture in all of our properties that empowers employees to serve our guests to the best of their ability.

United Hospitality Management and its founders are affiliated with an array of companies and industries such as real estate development and management, wholesale and distribution, restaurant management, as well as convenience store and gas station management. Our overall business experience has allowed us to deliver exceptional quality and value to all of our customers and partners.
